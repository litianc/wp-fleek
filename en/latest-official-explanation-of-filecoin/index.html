For customers: use block rewards to subsidize reliable storage

The purpose of filecoin network is to provide reliable and useful storage for clients. In the traditional workload proof, blockchain miners can choose to go offline freely at any time. In this case, they no longer earn large rewards, but are not affected. In contrast, filecoin miners must provide their customers with continuous storage services according to their contractual agreements. If a filecoin miner is offline while signing a contract with a customer, the service value of the customer will be damaged, and the health and reputation of the network will be damaged.
