In the discussion of the calculation model, it is assumed that the rational transaction sender will not set more than the gas burning threshold, but in reality, the official setting has the irrational result of gas over burning.As a sister part of [《Filecoin 手续费模型-EIP1559》](https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/234700165), this paper further analyzes the excess combustion in filecoin’s service charge model, and introduces a method to reduce the excess combustion charge.

Review of handling charge of filecoin

In the previous article, we introduced the scheme of eip-1559 adopted by filecoin to solve the problem of maliciously setting gaslimit. We analyze the calculation model and the main influencing factors of transaction fees from the macro level.

At that time, due to the limited understanding, gaslimit was directly applied to the concept of （gasToBurn）, but such a description was not accurate. Theoretically, the calculated result is close to the actual fil consumption, but there will be a maximum deviation of 10% under certain circumstances. This deviation is what we call excess burning cost.

Then, why and under what circumstances will there be excessive combustion?

The origin of excessive combustion

We know that gas is the amount of fuel consumed by the resources on the filecoin chain, which is paid by the transaction sender. It is similar to XX liters of gasoline consumed by a car for a certain distance.

Similar to Ethereum, Filecoin’s gas also has the concepts of GasLimit and GasUsed。

– GasLimit：Indicates the maximum amount of fuel consumed in this transaction, which is set by the transaction sender.
– GasUsed：Represents the amount of fuel consumed by the transaction on the chain, which is calculated after the transaction on the chain.

Unlike Ethereum, the sum of GasLimit of all messages contained in the Filecoin block must not exceed BlockGasLimit;Ethereum is the sum of GasUsed statistics of all messages.

>An interesting knowledge point:Because Filecoin’s blockchain adopts DAG structure, a Tipset can have multiple blocks, and the block order at the same height is counted by the block of the next Tipset.Therefore, GasUsed generated by the transaction can only be accurately calculated after the next Tipset block is chained.

Because of the difference of design, Filecoin needs to design Gas more complex, so it has the concept of Gas excess combustion.The design of Gas excess combustion appeared before EIP-1559 went online,It does not conflict with EIP-1559 , and can be regarded as a more basic Gas model rule than EIP-1559 .

Calculation of excess combustion

**Gas excess combustion（GasOverestimationBurn）**:When there is a large difference between GasLimit and GasUsed, the amount of extra Gas needed to be burned.

According to GasLimit and GasUsed, we divide Gas into three cases:

*（In order to facilitate modeling, the evaluation coefficient is assumed k = GasLimit / GasUsed）*

1. when GasLimit is close to GasUsed(1 <= k < 1.1),It is considered that the GasLimit setting is reasonable:

“`
GasOverestimationBurn = 0
“`

2. The GasLimit is larger than GasUsed (k >= 2.1),It is considered that the GasLimit setting is unreasonable:

“`
GasOverestimationBurn = GasLimit – GasUsed
“`

3. When GasLimit is between the two ( 1.1 <= k < 2.1),It is considered that this is a transitional range, which is fitted by parabola:

“`
GasOverestimationBurn = (GasLimit – 1.1* GasUsed) * (GasLimit – GasUsed) / GasUsed
GasOverestimationBurn = (k – 1.1) * (k – 1) * GasUsed
“`

Next, the control variable method is used,hypothesis GasUsed=30000，The horizontal axis is k,and the vertical axis is GasOverestimationBurn,The Gas excess combustion curve is obtained as follows.

GasLimit is set, the higher the excess combustion is;The closer GasLimitis to GasUsed,the less excess combustion,Under reasonable GasLimit conditions, the excess combustion is 0.

Under normal circumstances, the Filecoin client only needs to set the GasLimit a little higher than the estimated Gas setting (blue range) to achieve “zero” excess combustion.But the reality is that the default coefficient given in the official code is k=1.25，Therefore, most transactions in the chain have incurred excessive combustion costs at this stage. Is this a feature or a bug? Let’s go on with the analysis.

Is this a feature or a bug?

Through the official community communication platform records and the problem tracking on GitHub, we finally determined that this was an official modification caused by a Bug. A [problem report] submitted by community members three months after the main line(https://github.com/filecoin-project/lotus/issues/5066).The report content is that there will be about 40% deviation in the evaluation of the gas of the transaction type of ProveCommitSector(Hereinafter referred to as the Prove transaction) under certain conditions, which leads to the Gas overflow and transaction error.In order to avoid the Gas overflow problem of Prove transaction, the official adjusted it repeatedly and finally set the default coefficient to 1.25(That is, 25% downward fluctuation space is reserved)。

In v1.4.2, after analyzing the data of three transaction types of self built nodes, we draw the following conclusions:

* The evaluation deviation of Prove trading fluctuates greatly, with a difference of 40% between the lowest and the highest;
* PreCommitSector(Hereinafter referred to as Precommit
)There is a certain deviation in precommitsector trading, but the deviation fluctuation is small;
* SubmitWindowedPoSt(Hereinafter referred to as WindowedPoSt)Transactions can be evaluated accurately.

Excess combustion optimization

The above three kinds of transactions are the most expensive transaction types in Filecoin network. If we can reduce the excessive combustion of these three types of transactions, and even achieve “zero” excess combustion, it can save a lot of expenses for miners.

Our basic idea to optimize the excess combustion is to realize it according to different transaction types. At present, there are PreCommit transaction and WindowedPoSt transaction which can directly realize “zero” excess combustion;For the Prove transaction, we need to wait until the code of the consensus part is perfect to achieve the optimal optimization.(Ps：The v1.5.0 upgrade of 3.4 has some changes, and the prove transaction data needs to be further measured）

Therefore, for different stages of miners, the operational optimization methods and optimization space are also different.

For the miners whose computing power is stable and who only need to send WindowedPoSt transactions, the optimization operation is relatively simple: you can directly adjust the evaluation coefficient GasLimitOverestimati in mpool,Until “zero” is over burned.

For the miners who are increasing their computing power, they need to weigh the benefits of Gas over burning and the risk of Gas spillover, and calculate the most suitable evaluation coefficient for their own nodes by combining the speed of new computing power and the historical data of transactions.

If you have the ability to modify the code, you can try to modify the evaluation coefficient of different transaction types in the source code of mpool, so as to realize the optimization of the current handling charge

Summary

Excess burning fee is the basic design at the beginning of the construction of Filecoin blockchain, and its implementation is not affected by the later EIP-1559.In theory, all transactions of Filecoin should be easy to avoid excessive combustion.At present, the government is improving the underlying data structure to reduce the bias of Gas evaluation of Prove transaction.We believe that the general situation of excessive combustion is only a short-term state, and this problem will be solved through technological upgrading. At that time, ecological application developers and users do not need to know the underlying complex conceptual logic, they can pay more attention to business design and product experience.
